# Jornal Tatu — Design System

**Logo & Brand Identity for a premium Brazilian investigative nature journal**

---

## Brand Philosophy

- **Minimalist & Accessible** — Clean lines, serious typography, zero visual clutter
- **Journalistic Credibility** — Feels trustworthy, investigative, high-investment quality (Airbnb/Guardian aesthetic)
- **Brazilian Identity** — Armadillo (tatu) is distinctly Brazilian; nature-focused journalism
- **Digital-First** — Scales beautifully on all sizes (social, web, print)

---

## Logo System

### Mark
**Minimalist armadillo silhouette**
- Head: Simple circle with two eyes + snout
- Body: 4 curved segments (representing shell plates) with decreasing opacity → sense of depth
- Tail: Elegant curve extending right
- Legs: 4 geometric rectangles with subtle rotation

**Color**: #1F2937 (dark gray, not pure black—softer, more premium)
**Proportions**: Square canvas (1:1 ratio), scales 16px to 2000px+
**Spacing**: Always maintain 20% clear space around mark

### Typography

**Primary Font**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Weights: 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold), 700 (bold)
- Usage: All body text, headlines, UI, taglines

**Logo Title**: "Jornal Tatu"
- Font: Inter 600 (semibold)
- Size: 48px (logo size); scales proportionally
- Letter-spacing: -0.5px (tight, premium feel)
- Color: #1F2937

**Tagline**: "NATUREZA • INVESTIGAÇÃO • BRASIL"
- Font: Inter 400
- Size: 14px
- Letter-spacing: 1px (caps-spaced)
- Color: #6B7280 (gray)

## Usage Guidelines

### ✅ Do
- Maintain clear space (20% of logo height minimum)
- Use #1F2937 on white backgrounds
- Scale proportionally (no stretching/squashing)
- Use high-contrast backgrounds (white, light gray)
- Keep tagline visible on social + marketing

### 🚫 Don't
- Change colors (unless #1F2937 → grayscale)
- Rotate, skew, or distort
- Add drop shadows or effects
- Remove clear space
- Use on busy/dark backgrounds without contrast check

## Accent Usage

**Purple (#7C3AED) sparingly:**
- CTA buttons (hover state: #6D28D9)
- Divider lines under headers
- Icon accents in navigation
- Quote marks in article text
- Links (underlined, #7C3AED)

**Never use more than 2-3 purple accents per page.**

---

## Premium Touches

1. **Negative space** — Content breathes. Big margins.
2. **Subtle borders** — #E5E7EB, 1px, for card separation
3. **No shadows** — Clean, modern, not skeuomorphic
4. **Perfect alignment** — Use design grids (8px)
5. **Consistent spacing** — 8px, 16px, 24px, 32px, 48px only
6. **Micro-interactions** — Hover effects (opacity, scale 1.02x, not instant)
7. **Typography hierarchy** — Weight + size, never both light
